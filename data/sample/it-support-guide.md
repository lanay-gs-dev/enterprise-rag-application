# IT Support Guide

Employees should submit IT support requests through the internal help desk.
Urgent access issues should be marked as high priority and include the affected
system, business impact, and preferred contact method.

Password resets, device issues, and software access requests are handled during
standard support hours unless the issue affects studio operations.
