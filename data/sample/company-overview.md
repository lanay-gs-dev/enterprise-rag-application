# Company Overview

Apex Wellness Studios operates wellness locations across the DC, Maryland, and
Virginia region. The company provides yoga, Pilates, barre, meditation, and
personal training services.

The company mission is to make evidence-informed wellness services accessible
to members through consistent operations, trained staff, and clear internal
policies.

This overview is approved for broad employee reference and does not contain
confidential operating metrics, customer records, or restricted security
procedures.
