# Vendor Risk Review

Vendors with access to customer data, payment systems, or employee records must
complete a security review before onboarding. Required evidence may include SOC
2 reports, data handling procedures, breach notification terms, and access
control documentation.

Vendor exceptions require Security approval, a risk owner, and an expiration
date for review.
