# Employee Handbook

Employees may work remotely up to three days per week with manager approval. Teams may define core collaboration hours based on business needs.

Full-time employees receive fifteen paid vacation days per calendar year. Unused vacation days do not roll over unless required by local law.

Employees should report security incidents to the Security team within one business day. Urgent incidents involving customer data should be escalated immediately.
